About Us:
Welcome to the virtual home of the D.B.M.S. English School Alumni Association (DESAA), where the bonds of friendship formed within our school walls extend far beyond graduation. At DBMS, we believe in fostering a community that not only celebrates our shared heritage and love for Jamshedpur but also strives to make a meaningful impact in the world.
Our Mission:
Driven by the ethos of our school anthem, "……to help all those in need,…" DESAA is dedicated to serving the greater good. We are committed to engaging in charity and social activities aimed at uplifting the lives of the poor and weaker sections of society. Through our collective efforts, we aim to bring hope, dignity, and positive change to those in need.
